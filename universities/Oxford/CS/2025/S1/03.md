## Overview

Linear Algebra provides essential mathematical foundations for computer science, covering vector spaces, matrices, eigenvalues, and linear transformations. This course is crucial for understanding graphics, machine learning, optimization, and numerical computation.

## Learning Objectives

- Master vector operations and vector spaces
- Understand matrix algebra and operations
- Compute eigenvalues and eigenvectors
- Apply linear transformations to practical problems
- Solve systems of linear equations

## Syllabus Outline

**Weeks 1-2**: Vectors and Vector Spaces
- Vector operations and properties
- Linear independence and basis
- Subspaces and spanning sets

**Weeks 3-4**: Matrices and Matrix Operations
- Matrix arithmetic and properties
- Matrix inverses and determinants
- Gaussian elimination and row operations

**Weeks 5-6**: Linear Transformations
- Linear maps between vector spaces
- Matrix representations of transformations
- Change of basis and similarity

**Weeks 7-8**: Eigenvalues and Applications
- Eigenvalue and eigenvector computation
- Diagonalization of matrices
- Applications to computer graphics and data analysis

## Assessment & Workload

Problem sets, tutorial exercises, and examinations focusing on both theoretical understanding and computational skills. Regular tutorials provide individual feedback.
